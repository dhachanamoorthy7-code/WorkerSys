Quick Getting Started Guide for Beginners

Here is how you can easily test this application in seconds:

  • Mark Attendance: Navigate to the Attendance Tracker tab on the left sidebar to mark workers as present/absent.
  • Simulate Scans: Try out the QR Simulator or Biometric Simulator tab to register scans using mockviewfinders.
  • Shift Board: Click Status Board in the sidebar to toggle workers on break or working in real-time.
  • Clean Database: If you delete data or want to reset the app, click the Reset Demo Data button below.
